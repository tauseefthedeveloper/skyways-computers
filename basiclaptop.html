Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. What are refurbished basic laptops?

These are corporate lease return laptops from premium tier-1 brands like Dell, HP, and Lenovo. They undergo a complete testing cycle at SKYWAYS COMPUTERS, ensuring flawless functional working order equal to brand new machines.

Q2. Do you offer wholesale or bulk deals?

Yes, absolutely. We are premier commercial IT asset distributors in Sakinaka, Mumbai. We entertain bulk stock orders for corporate deployments, schools, call-centers, and retail computer shop re-sellers at dealer prices.

Q3. Can I test the laptop before buying?

Definitely! You can visit our physical shop located at 90 Feet Road near Sakinaka, Mumbai, to completely test physical aesthetics, internal hardware, display quality, and battery health before making a final payment.

Q4. What accessories come with the product?

Every basic refurbished laptop purchased from SKYWAYS COMPUTERS comes carefully packaged along with its certified genuine compatible diagnostic power adapter charger, ready to plug-and-play immediately.
