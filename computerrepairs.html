Frequently Asked Questions (Desktop Repairs)

Q1. How much time does a typical desktop repair take?

Standard issues like RAM/SSD dynamic upgrades, system software configuration, or original SMPS setups are executed on the same day. Intensive chip-level motherboard logic traces might require 24 to 48 hours for exhaustive calibration.

Q2. Do you provide a warranty on replaced components?

Yes, absolutely. All genuine replacement components used at SKYWAYS COMPUTER—including solid-state storage drives, modular RAM units, and power supply arrays—come along with direct verified standard warranty bounds.

Q3. Can your team fix a computer that won't turn on at all?

Yes, we specialize in dead system diagnostics. Our senior tech engineers track electrical pathways across the mainboards to isolate capacitors shorts or circuit blowouts and bring your computer desktop operational status back to life.

Q4. Where is your physical service lab located in Mumbai?

Our official main hardware workshop is based at Shop No. 4, 90 Feet Road, Mohili Village, Sakinaka, Mumbai (Opposite Yari D'souza Nagar), optimized with state-of-the-art tech instrumentation desks.
